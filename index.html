Sword

Sword is a great beginner's weapon. It deals moderate damage with moderate attack speed. The sword also has a few varieties of attack combinations. Great for breaking parts off behemoths.

Hammer

Hammer is a great weapon of choice for those looking to challenge themselves. Deals a great amount of damage per hit. Attack speed is on the slower side so you must pick your timing to attack. Hammer is your go to weapon if you want to stagger the behemoths.

Blade

Blade also know as chain blades. Lightning quick attack speed. Light attacks but with constant damage, definitly top damage provider. Has close to medium attack range. Using the blade provides the ability to constantly harass the behemoth for every second it is still breathing.

Axe

The axe is for those hard hitting lovers. Has the ability to wind up for a charged attack that deals devastating damage to the behemoth. Deals primarily part damage but still deals a great amount of stagger damage as well.

Pike

War pike, quick continous combination for consecutive damaging. The pike is great for weakening the behemoth with wound damage. The pike is also capable of firing off a long distance cannon shot.
